Tamaar Padaar Role Play Server

Tamaar Padaar is a new Malayalam Role play server which incorporates classic role playing techniques with new scripts
and scope for creative role playing. This server is run by a bunch of players who have played in various Malayalam RP
servers and have left them due to various issues including power gaming and harassment. Since most of us know what
it’s like to be on the receiving end of this, we make sure that none of this would hamper your role playing experience.
We aim to give primary importance to roleplay but that doesn’t mean we’re conservative about the scripts we add. We
guarantee you an immersive and gratifying experience.

Whats new!

The server is crafted in such a way that role play is given prime importance. Each element
in this server requires high-quality roleplay. Apart from having an automated driving school,
we have an RTO to check and provide license, vehicle registration, etc. There is also a car
dealership with a car dealer and financing options. There is also a lot of diverse jobs and
events. So join TPRP and explore the enhanced roleplay experience. If you have suggestions
let us know...
